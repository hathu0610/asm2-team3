Assignment 2: The IT World

An assignment for class COSC2083 Intro to IT about how the IT Work, Technologies and our team's Project Ideas

Team Profile

Thu Nguyen

Profile

My name is Nguyen Thi Ha Thu (s3927104), a first-year student at RMIT Vietnam. My journey into technology began when I first started university in the US working on my major in economics. As I was taking my fundamental classes, I learned Computer Science 101 and met a teacher who had a deep passion for tech and aspired to pass on his passion to his students - including me. He viewed coding as ‘mysteries', and we - the coder - were the “detective" who needed to solve those mysteries. In the summer of my second year, I was lucky enough to get a job at a game studio where I joined as a Game Developer Intern. I had a wonderful experience with my team as well as being mentored by my dedicated Tech Lead. In contrast to what I hoped, I did not feel satisfied and enjoyable while being a Developer and decided to take a gap year. Coming back to Viet Nam, I found a suitable job that combines my passion for Tech with my capability in Analysis/Operation - to work as a Product Owner for an e-commerce company. I noticed that due to a lack in my knowledge about Tech, this created a gap between me and the Tech team since my job heavily involves working between Developers, Designers, and the Business team. I decided to continue my learning journey at RMIT since RMIT has the best education quality with an English-speaking environment. I believe that RMIT will equip me with enough knowledge for my future job and learn about how technology will change in the future.

ESFP

Career Plan

The job that I aimed for in the future is being Product Manager at Tiki. For the position, the ideal candidate must have a customer-first mindset, a strong understanding of back-end and front-end programming languages, and the ability to leverage big data to develop, analyze, and improve our financial products. This role requires a candidate with curiosity, excellent analytical, organizational, and communication skills as he/she‘ll be working with many different constituents. This is a customer-centric role, working to translate the needs of the customer into online financial product opportunities.
